Tools and techniques to help manage stress and anxiety.

Stress Relief Techniques

🫁

Deep Breathing

Slow, intentional breathing activates your body's relaxation response. Try inhaling for 4 counts, holding for 4, exhaling for 6.

🍅

Pomodoro Technique

Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat. This prevents burnout and keeps your brain fresh throughout the day.

🧘

Mindfulness

Spend 5 minutes focusing only on your senses — what you see, hear, feel. Anchoring yourself in the present reduces anxiety.

📓

Journaling

Writing down your thoughts externalises them, making them feel more manageable. Even 3 sentences a day can help.

🚶

Short Walks

A 10-minute walk boosts mood-regulating chemicals in your brain. Step outside — even a short loop around campus helps.

📵

Screen Breaks

Every 60 minutes, step away from your screen. Look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain and mental fatigue.
